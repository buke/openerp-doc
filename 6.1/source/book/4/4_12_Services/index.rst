Deliver Quality Services
************************

.. i18n:  *This chapter focuses on the management of contracts, and the services associated with that.
.. i18n:  The complete process of managing services is studied here: from defining prices and contracts to
.. i18n:  automatically invoicing the services through planning, and the treatment of
.. i18n:  additional costs such as expense receipts.*
.. i18n:  
.. i18n: For this chapter you should start with a fresh database that includes demo data,

.. i18n: no particular chart of accounts configured.

 *This chapter focuses on the management of contracts, and the services associated with that.
 The complete process of managing services is studied here: from defining prices and contracts to
 automatically invoicing the services through planning, and the treatment of
 additional costs such as expense receipts.*
 
For this chapter you should start with a fresh database that includes demo data,
with :mod:`sale`, :mod:`project` and all of their dependencies installed, and 
no particular chart of accounts configured.
